About Me

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D student in ShanghaiTech University (2019.09-today), majoring in Computer Science. I am a member of System and Software Security Lab (S3L) and advised by Prof. Fu Song, the PI of S3L.

I received my Bachelor's degree (2015.09-2019.06, major: Information Engineering) in South China University of Technology, Guangzhou. 💫
